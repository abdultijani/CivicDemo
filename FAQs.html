Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about how CivicNow works, how your representatives are identified, and how your information is handled.

About CivicNow

What is CivicNow?

CivicNow helps Massachusetts residents quickly find their elected representatives at the local, state, and federal levels.

Who is CivicNow for?

CivicNow is designed for Massachusetts residents who want an easier way to understand their representation and participate in civic life.

Is CivicNow affiliated with the government?

No. CivicNow is an informational civic engagement platform and is not a government agency or affiliated with any political party.

Using the Platform

How does CivicNow find my representatives?

When you enter your address, CivicNow identifies the government districts associated with that location and matches them with the officials who represent those districts.

What representatives will I see?

You may see representatives from several levels of government:

  • U.S. House of Representatives
  • U.S. Senators
  • Massachusetts State Representatives
  • Massachusetts State Senators
  • Local officials when available
Can I use CivicNow outside Massachusetts?

The current version of CivicNow focuses on Massachusetts residents. Future versions may expand to other states.

Privacy & Data

Is my address stored?

No. CivicNow does not permanently store addresses entered into the search tool.
